Who's that meat?

I'm going to style this page later, but for now I'll just explain what this does:

Drag this bookmarklet to your bar. Then, in meatspaces or facespaces, click it when you're not sure who's saying something. It will add a colored square to each user so you can line it up easy, rather than mousing over and looking at fingerprints.
